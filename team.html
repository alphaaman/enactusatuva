Our Team

The driving force behind Enactus at UVa!

Declan Mawer, President

Declan Mawer is a third-year student studying Commerce and Economics. He began his involvement with Enactus as a Project Leader at the beginning of the 2014-2015 school year and is now the President. He is also involved in Madison House as a Program Director and Student Member of the Board of Directors, a member of the Community Service Council for the Inter-Fraternity Council, and Philanthropy Chair for Kappa Sigma Fraternity.

Miranda Wang, VP of Technology

Miranda Wang is a fourth-year studying Computer Science and Psychology in the College of Arts and Sciences at the University of Virginia. She is currently the Vice President of Technology for Enactus at UVa, aspiring to be a web designer and front end developer, with interests in technology consulting, marketing, and user experience design. She has worked in the past with student organizations such as Virginia Homecomings and Corks & Curls, the yearbook at UVa, to create websites and sculpt their online presences.

Sunshine Yao, VP of Marketing

Sunshine Yao is a third-year student studying Finance and Marketing at the University of Virginia. She joined Enactus her first year as a project member for the Center for Traditional Textiles of Cusco (CTTC), and became the project leader the next year. She is currently the VP of Marketing and is responsible for all publicity materials. She is also the Senior Advisor and Publicity chair for international cultural organizations like Mainland Student Network and LingXi Chinese Drama Club.

Pooja Ranganathan, VP of Finance

Pooja Ranganathan is a third-year student studying Financial Economics in the College of Arts and Sciences. Her involvement with Enactus began through the Women Economic Empowerment (WEE) team, where she joined as a project member. She is now Vice President of Finance for Enactus at UVA. In addition to this role, she is a research assistant at the Darden School of Business and Minister of Community Operations at the International Residential College.

Jonathan Qian, Co-VP of Business Relations

Jonathan Qian is a second-year at UVa planning on majoring in Finance and Statistics. He is currently the Co-Vice President of Business Relations of Enactus at UVA and served as a project member in the Center for Traditional Textiles in Cusco Project his first year. In addition to Enactus, Jonathan is also an analyst in the McIntire Investment Institute and serves as the Finance Officer for ULink.

Sebastian Kingston, Co-VP of Business Relations
